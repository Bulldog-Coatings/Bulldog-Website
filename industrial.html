Scientific Applications

Labs

Floor coatings are an essential part of any laboratory. They are designed to provide a safe and hygienic surface that is easy to clean and maintain. They should also be able to resist chemicals, bacteria, and other contaminants that may be present in the lab environment. The right floor coating can make all the difference in a lab setting, providing both antimicrobial protection and chemical resistance while still being low maintenance and easy to clean. In this article, we will explore the different types of floor coatings available for laboratory floors, their benefits, and how they can help ensure a safe working environment for everyone.

Clean Rooms

High performance floor coatings are essential in any clean room environment. It helps to keep the environment free from dust and other contaminants, while providing a low maintenance and easy to clean surface. Antimicrobial coatings are standard to the flooring, making it even easier to keep the area hygienic.
