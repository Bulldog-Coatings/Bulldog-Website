About Metallic Finish

Metallic flooring systems from Bulldog Coatings offer the ultimate in resinous flooring design and customization. They provide owners with a unique finish with an infinite number of colors and blends. Additional colors may also be incorporated to help accent the floor. Our Metallic flooring systems consist of suspended metallic pigments within an industrial grade body coat and Aliphatic Polyurea top coats to provide a spectacular finish that can be customized for any setting. A perfect selection for commercial environments and retail showrooms, decorative metallic flooring offers a truly one of a kind floor system that your customers will remember.
