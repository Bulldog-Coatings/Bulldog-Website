Exterior Applications

Garage Floors

Our garage floor coatings are not only functional, but they are a long-lasting protectant for your garage floor. They're professionally applied by our team of experienced installers so you always get the promised result! Our professional grade Polyurea floor coatings have many benefits for the homeowner. These include a cleaner looking floor, a lightly textured non-slip surface, and an extended living space. The coating protects from anything that would come off the street or your vehicle, such as salt or oil, and are UV stable.

Patios

Patios are exposed to the elements and Minnesota weather patterns. Our coatings are designed to withstand these conditions and protect your concrete from them. We are using exterior rated Polyurea coatings that are UV Stable and resist fading. By coating your exterior concrete, you are protecting it for years to come!

Pool Decks

Our Full Chip coating system is a great choice for pool decks because of its anti-slip properties and its ability to protect the concrete from harsh chemicals. We have many colors to choose from whether you want a natural stone or a bright tropical color. Let us protect your pool deck and enhance the appearance of your outdoor space!
