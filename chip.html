About

Perfect for any garage, patio, basement, or commercial floor! The versatility and strength of our high-quality chip system create a clean, beautiful look to all of your high traffic areas. Our chip system uses 100% solids polyurea with three distinct layers of industrial strength protection and durability. These floors are customized with a blend of vinyl flakes to give your floor the look of granite or terrazzo. With a return to service in as little as 24 hours, you can be back to using your space in no time.
